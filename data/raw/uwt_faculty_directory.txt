University of Washington Tacoma School of Engineering and Technology

Computer Science and Systems faculty teach courses related to programming, software engineering, databases, algorithms, networking, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and data science.

Faculty members support student learning through lectures, office hours, research opportunities, and project supervision.

The department emphasizes practical experience through team projects, software development practices, and industry-relevant technologies.

Students are encouraged to meet with faculty members to discuss coursework, internships, research opportunities, and career planning.

The program combines theoretical foundations with hands-on technical skills.
